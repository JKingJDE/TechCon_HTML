History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of developers and innovators. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the largest global technology conferences, attracting thousands of participants annually.

The mission of TechCon is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and drive technological progress that positively impacts the world.
